On Base, a deposit should actually cost **less than $0.05**. When MetaMask shows a fee like $3.00 or $6.00 and says **"This transaction is likely to fail,"** it means the Smart Contract has **reverted** (rejected) your transaction. 

Because the transaction is failing, MetaMask cannot calculate the gas correctly, so it shows a "maximum safe limit" or an arbitrary high number as a warning. **Do not confirm this transaction while the warning is there.**

---

Based on your screenshots, there are three likely reasons:
